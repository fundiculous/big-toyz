2017 Guinness World Record-Longest Monster Truck

Click the logo above to see the official record

2017 was an exciting year for the Sin City Hustler. The Guinness World Record crew came out for a shoot, present the award, and dub the truck the "World's Longest Monster Truck" allowing it to go down in history. The Hustler was featured both in the annual 2017 book (p 99), the 2018 book, as well as through social media and numerous web channels.
